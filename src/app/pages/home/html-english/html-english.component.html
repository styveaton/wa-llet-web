Personal information. Register by providing your full name, your email address, mobile phone number, gender, date of birth and address.

Transfer information.Choose the amount you want to send, select the method of receipt desired by the beneficiary, as well as the method of payment. We will always tell you the exact amount the beneficiary will receive.

Recipient information. We will need specific contact details relating to the beneficiary abroad. The beneficiary's full name, mobile phone number are still required for sending money. Please note that the beneficiary is not required to have a Wa-llet account.

Payment information. You will need to top up your Wa-llet account using Zelle so you can send money abroad
